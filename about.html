About the Paper

Founded in 2026 by a group of journalists who could not take it anymore, News Tabloid Now is the internet's most trusted source of completely fabricated news. We believe that in a world where reality is already absurd, the only responsible thing to do is make it slightly more absurd.

What started as a group chat where five reporters would vent about increasingly unbelievable real headlines has evolved into a full-scale publication dedicated to the fine art of deadpan absurdism. Every article you read here is crafted with the same editorial rigor you would expect from a Pulitzer-winning newsroom, except everything is made up and the facts do not matter.

We hold ourselves to the highest journalistic standards of fiction. Every fake quote is carefully attributed to a fake expert. Every fabricated statistic is presented with complete confidence. Every story is workshopped until it achieves that perfect balance of "this is ridiculous" and "wait, is this real?"

Our Mission

To make you laugh, think, and then wonder if maybe our version of events is actually more plausible than what is really happening.

We believe satire is not just comedy. It is a public service. In an era where the line between news and fiction has been blurred beyond recognition, we figured we might as well have fun on this side of it. If you are going to read something that sounds too insane to be true, it might as well be written by professionals.
