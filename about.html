About the Paper

News Tabloid Now was founded in 2026 by a former government whistleblower and a competitive fisherman who saw something in the lake. They have not agreed on what it was. They have agreed that the public deserves to know about it, whatever it is.

We cover what the mainstream media will not: aliens, time travel, cryptids, ghosts, and the occasional sentient robot. While other publications concern themselves with elections, economics, and geopolitical stability, we focus on what actually matters. A grey alien was elected mayor of a town in Ohio. Bigfoot works for the IRS. The moon is made of cheese. These are the stories that keep people up at night, and we intend to keep it that way.

Our editorial standards are rigorous. Every source is vetted, every quote confirmed, and every photograph examined for signs of tampering. If we cannot verify a claim with at least two independent witnesses, a blurry photograph, and a sworn affidavit from someone who seems mostly credible, we do not run the story. We have a rejection rate of nearly 4%.

Our Mission

To report the truth that other outlets are too afraid, too compromised, or too "rational" to cover. We believe the American public is smart enough to decide for themselves whether a 7-foot alien should run their town's infrastructure program. Spoiler: they decided yes.

We also believe in accountability. When we make an error, we print a correction. We have printed many corrections. We stand by all of them, including the one where we accidentally referred to the Loch Ness Monster as "Larry." His name is not Larry. We apologize to the creature and to Larry, who is a separate individual and also a plumber in Duluth.
